Delicious Sushi Recipe

Homemade Sushi is so much cheaper than at the restaurant. Plus, it’s easy and fun
to make your own rolls at home, so you can put all your favorite ingredients into your perfect custom roll — here’s how!

Ingredients

  • 6 sheets sushi seaweed aka nori
  • 1 batch prepared sushi rice
  • 1/2 lb sashimi-grade raw salmon or desired raw fish of choice
  • 4 oz cream cheese sliced into strips
  • 1 avocado sliced
  • soy sauce for serving
